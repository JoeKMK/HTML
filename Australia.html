Australia

Wikipedia

Australia is a beautiful country and continent
surrounded by the Indian and Pacific oceans.
Its major cities - Sydney, Brisbane, Melbourne, Perth, and Adelaide - are coastal.
On the country's east coast is the Great Barrier Reef, a vast system of coral reefs.
There are dolphins and sharks too.

Cities

Sydney is the state capital of New South Wales and the most populous city in Australia and Oceania.

Landmarks

The Sydney Opera House is a multi-venue performing arts centre in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia. It is one of the 20th century's most famous and distinctive buildings.

Sports

Australian rules football, known colloquially as footy, is a contact sport played between two teams of eighteen players on an oval-shaped field.

Food

Fish and chips is a delicious Australian take-away food. It is a hot dish of fried battered fish and hot chips. Vegemite is another famous dark brown Australian food spread with various vegetable and spice additives.

Animals

The kangaroo is a marsupial from the family Macropodidae (macropods, meaning "large foot").
